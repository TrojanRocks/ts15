Techspardha
2014-15, NIT Kurukshetra
Techspardha is annual technical fest of NIT Kurukshetra to be held on 19th-22nd March, 2015. Techspardha attracts a large number of students with its outstanding events and competitions.

Techspardha is announcing the Student Campus Ambassador registrations. Student Campus Ambassadors are enthusiastic students who help us to reach out their colleges and promote technology.
