Excalibur

Excalibur is one of the flagship events of Techspardha. It is a software development event. It is a team event with maximum of four members per team. The team needs to prepare a proposal for the idea. Proposal consists of following Abstract - It will represent what the idea is all about. Flow - Flow chart of each of the use cases. Diagrams can be used. One email address for the communication Name of all team members Points to be considered Idea should be unique or innovative. Keep in mind - ‘Do the different or do the same by different way’. Any programming language or platform can be used. After the idea is selected for Excalibur, you will be assigned a mentor. Mentor will guide you in development of the software. Before final day of the event, shortlisting will be done on the basis of project completion. Teams will be required to show the demo. The shortlisted teams will be required to give demo and presentation on their software explaining the features, why and how they do it. There will be two winners on the basis of idea, project and presentation and they will be awarded prizes worth Rs. 5000 and some great surprise deal. Last date of Proposal Submission - February 5, 2015.
